# Bright Smile Dental — Family Booking Guide

## Back-to-Back Family Appointments

We offer back-to-back scheduling for families so everyone can be seen on the same day with minimal waiting. When booking for multiple family members, let us know so we can arrange consecutive time slots.

- **Up to 4 consecutive slots** can be reserved for a single family visit.
- We recommend scheduling all family members on the same day for maximum convenience.

## Children Under 18

- A **parent or legal guardian must be present** during all appointments for patients under 18 years of age.
- Insurance for minors is typically filed under the parent's or guardian's plan.

## Insurance Shortcut

If all family members are covered under the **same insurance plan**, let us know upfront. We can apply the same coverage details to each family member's booking, speeding up the check-in process.

## Tips for a Smooth Family Visit

1. **Book early in the day** — morning appointments tend to have the shortest wait times and work well for young children.
2. **Bring one insurance card** if the whole family is on the same plan — we will copy it for each patient's file.
3. **Arrive 15 minutes early** for the first appointment if any family member is a new patient (paperwork).
4. **Let us know ages of children** when scheduling so we can allocate the right amount of time and the appropriate provider.
5. **Mention any special needs** (wheelchair access, sensory sensitivities, language preference) when booking so we can prepare.
