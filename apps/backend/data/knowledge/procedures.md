# Bright Smile Dental — Procedures & Services

## Preventive Care

### Cleaning (Prophylaxis)
- **Duration:** approximately 30 minutes
- Routine cleaning to remove plaque and tartar buildup. Recommended every 6 months.

### Deep Cleaning / Scaling & Root Planing
- **Duration:** approximately 60-90 minutes
- Treatment for gum disease (periodontal disease). Removes bacteria and tartar below the gumline. May require local anesthesia.

## Diagnostic

### Checkup / Comprehensive Exam
- **Duration:** approximately 30 minutes
- Includes oral examination, digital X-rays if needed, oral cancer screening, and treatment plan discussion.

### Consultation
- **Duration:** approximately 20 minutes
- Meet with the dentist to discuss specific concerns, review treatment options, or get a second opinion.

## Restorative

### Filling
- **Duration:** approximately 30-60 minutes
- Tooth-colored composite fillings to repair cavities. Duration depends on the number and size of fillings.

### Crown
- **Duration:** 2 visits, approximately 60 minutes each
- Visit 1: Tooth preparation, impression, and temporary crown placement.
- Visit 2: Permanent crown fitting and cementation.

### Root Canal
- **Duration:** approximately 60-90 minutes
- Removes infected pulp tissue from inside the tooth, followed by sealing. A crown is usually recommended afterward.

### Extraction
- **Duration:** approximately 30-60 minutes
- Removal of a tooth that cannot be saved. Includes simple and surgical extractions.

## Cosmetic

### Teeth Whitening
- **Duration:** approximately 60 minutes (in-office)
- Professional-grade whitening for noticeably brighter results in a single visit.

### Veneers
- **Duration:** 2 visits required
- Custom porcelain shells bonded to the front of teeth for a transformed smile.

## Implants & Prosthetics

### Dental Implants
- Multi-visit procedure offered at our office.
- Titanium post placed in the jawbone to replace a missing tooth root, topped with a custom crown.

### Night Guards (Bruxism)
- Custom-fitted mouth guard to protect teeth from grinding during sleep.
- Requires an impressions appointment followed by a fitting appointment.

## Referral-Only Services

The following services require a referral to a specialist:

- **Dentures and partials** — referred to a prosthodontist.
- **Orthodontics** — referred to an orthodontist, except for Dr. Michael Chen's Tuesday consultation hours at our office.

*Note: All procedure durations are approximate and may vary based on individual patient needs.*
