# Bright Smile Dental — Insurance & Payment Policy

## Accepted Insurance Plans

We are in-network with the following dental insurance providers:

- Delta Dental
- Aetna
- Cigna
- Blue Cross Blue Shield (BCBS)
- MetLife
- Guardian
- United Healthcare

We verify your insurance benefits before your visit so there are no surprises.

## Self-Pay & Uninsured Patients

- **15% discount** for uninsured patients who pay in full at the time of service.
- **CareCredit financing** is available for patients who prefer to spread payments over time.

## Bright Smile Membership Plan

For patients without insurance, our annual membership plan offers excellent value:

- **Cost:** $299 per year
- **Includes:**
  - 2 professional cleanings
  - 1 comprehensive exam
  - Full set of digital X-rays
  - 20% discount on all other procedures

## Payment & Billing

- Payment is due at the time of service.
- We accept cash, checks, all major credit cards, and HSA/FSA cards.
- We file insurance claims on your behalf — you only pay your estimated co-pay or deductible at checkout.
- If your insurance pays more or less than estimated, we will refund or bill the difference.
